Frequently Asked Questions

What is Longevity Cash (LGCX)? +
LGCX is a cryptocurrency themed around longevity and healthy lifespan. It is a fixed-supply, non-custodial asset you can hold, trade and use for privacy-conscious payments in and around the longevity economy.
Why might someone hold LGCX? +
LGCX lets you express a simple thesis: if longevity technologies, clinics and services grow, a native, scarce asset associated with that narrative and usage may benefit from increased demand. It also offers self-custody, pseudonymous ownership and global transferability. None of this is a guarantee of returns or investment advice.
How private is it to use? +
Like other Base- and Ethereum-based assets, LGCX is pseudonymous: addresses are visible on-chain, but you control how you link them to your real-world identity. Using non-custodial wallets, good operational hygiene and privacy-preserving tools can further reduce your footprint.
Which network is it on? +
LGCX is issued on Base, an Ethereum Layer 2 built by Coinbase. This provides low fees, fast confirmations and broad compatibility with existing DeFi tooling.
